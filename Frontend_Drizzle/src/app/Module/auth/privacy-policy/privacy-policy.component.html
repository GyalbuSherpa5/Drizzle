We use the term "Personal Information" to describe information that can be associated with a particular person and can be used to identify that person. Personal Information includes the information described in this section, including your Financial Information. We use the term “Financial Information” to describe information tied to financial accounts and services, such as your bank account numbers or payment card information. We do not consider Personal Information to include anonymized information unless any applicable law provides otherwise.

If you open an Account or use DRIZZLE Services, we may collect or use the following types of information:

Contact or identifiers: Your name, address, phone, date of birth, social security number, email address, third-party application IDs, One Time Passwords (OTPs), passwords, Personal Identification Numbers (PINs), biometric IDs, and other similar information.

Financial Information: Bank account numbers and payment card numbers and related information for accounts or cards that you link to your Account or give us when you use DRIZZLE Services.

Computer, phone or other device information: Your computer IP address; your device ID or unique identifier; your device type; geolocation information; computer and connection information; mobile network information; statistics on page views; traffic to and from the sites; referral URLs; ad data; standard web log data and other similar information (see Section 4 regarding cookies).

Communications: The information you enter when you add data to a web form, add or update your account information, participate in chats or dispute resolutions, or when you otherwise correspond with us regarding DRIZZLE Services or other information regarding any contact you have with any of our services or employees.
